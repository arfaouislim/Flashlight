Privacy Policy for Flashlight

Effective Date: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Flashlight mobile application (including the standard flashlight, SOS light, and Morse code light features) created and operated by sa.devstudios ("Service Provider", "we", "us", or "our").

Summary: Flashlight is a utility application designed to operate your device's LED flash/camera light. We do not require account registration, and we do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information directly.

1. Information Collection and Account Sign-In

The Application is built as a simple utility tool:

  • No User Accounts / Sign-In: You are not required to create an account, register, or provide any personal details to use the Application.
  • No Direct Data Collection: The Service Provider does not collect or store any personal data, location details, contacts, or media from your device.

2. Camera Permission

To operate the physical LED flashlight, SOS light, and Morse code light functions, the Application requires access to your device's camera hardware (specifically the LED flash unit). The Application does not record audio, take photos, or record videos.

3. Advertising & Third-Party Services (Google AdMob)

The Application is supported by advertising and utilizes Google AdMob to display advertisements. While the Service Provider does not collect personal data, Google AdMob and its partners may automatically collect non-personally identifiable device information to serve relevant ads, prevent fraud, and measure ad performance.

Information collected by AdMob may include:

  • Advertising ID (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and general coarse location data
  • Device identifiers, operating system version, and performance diagnostics

For more details on how Google handles advertising data, please review:

  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services
  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy

4. Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). Because no personal information is requested or stored by the Application, it remains safe for general audiences.

5. Opt-Out & Uninstallation

You can stop all function and potential ad-related data transmission by simply uninstalling the Application from your device using standard mobile app management tools.
